Our Story

About ChaatBasket

ChaatBasket started from a simple idea — that everyone deserves delicious, affordable street food made with love and the freshest ingredients. What began as a humble stall has grown into a neighbourhood favourite, serving smiles one crunchy bite at a time.

We believe in keeping things real — no artificial colours, no stale ingredients, just honest-to-goodness chaat that reminds you of home. Every bowl is handcrafted with care, because this isn't just food — it's an experience.

Thank you for being part of our journey. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a loyal regular, you make ChaatBasket what it is.
